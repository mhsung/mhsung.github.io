About Me

I'm a Research Scientist at Adobe Research. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science department at Stanford University advised by Leonidas Guibas. My research focuses on 3D deep learning and geometry processing. Previously, I have interned at Google, Adobe Research, and Autodesk Research, and worked as a researcher at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST). I received MS and BS degrees in Computer Science at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST).
